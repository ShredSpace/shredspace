Appel Games

Appel

Play

Appel is a platformer game about running and jumping, collecting red and golden apples, and reaching the goal of the level. The original game is very easy for beginners.

Appel Desert

Play

Appel Desert is a hard set of custom levels for Appel. It is intended for after you beat the original. The levels are desert-themed and fairly difficult.

Absurd Appel

Play

Absurd Appel is a set of extremely hard levels for Appel, requiring glitches, tricks, and tons of skill to master. Not recommended for beginners.

Volcanic Appel

Play

Appel Volcano is an easy set of custom levels designed for fun and speedrunning after you beat the original Appel. Some levels get more difficult, and it follows a difficulty curve.

Appel Community Edition

Play

Appel Community Edition is a mod of Appel that adds more features not allowed on Scratch.
